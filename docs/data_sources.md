# Data sources

## Must-have sources

### 1. AI pilot zones

- CSET first 11 zones: https://cset.georgetown.edu/publication/china-creates-national-new-generation-artificial-intelligence-innovation-and-development-pilot-zones/
- Xinhua 17-zone confirmation by Dec 2021: https://www.news.cn/2021-12/06/c_1128137133.htm
- MOST guidance: https://www.most.gov.cn/xxgk/xinxifenlei/fdzdgknr/fgzc/gfxwj/gfxwj2020/202012/t20201224_171987.html

Use: treatment table.

### 2. Smart factories

- Official 2024 first-batch excellence smart-factory notice: https://www.ncsti.gov.cn/kjdt/tzgg/202501/t20250103_191636.html
- Local confirmation of 235 first-batch projects and four-tier gradient cultivation: https://www.nanjing.gov.cn/zt/sznjjsqx/cgzs/202501/t20250107_5049172.html
- 2025 public notice mirror: https://finance.sina.com.cn/jjxw/2025-09-05/doc-infpkxsn6100890.shtml

Use: primary adoption measure.

### 3. BACI exports

- BACI main page: https://www.cepii.fr/CEPII/en/bdd_modele/bdd_modele_item.asp?id=37
- BACI documentation: https://www.cepii.fr/DATA_DOWNLOAD/baci/doc/baci_webpage.html

Use: HS6 export value, quantity, unit value, market-share outcomes.

### 4. City controls

- EPS China City Statistics: https://epschinastats.com/db_city.html
- NBS China Statistical Yearbooks fallback: https://www.stats.gov.cn/sj/ndsj/

Use: city-year controls and outcomes.

## Optional validation sources

### AI patents

- CSET 1790 AI patent data: https://github.com/georgetown-cset/1790-ai-patent-data
- DeepInnovationAI: https://figshare.com/articles/dataset/_/28578947

### Robotics

- IFR World Robotics press release: https://ifr.org/ifr-press-releases/news/global-robot-demand-in-factories-doubles-over-10-years

Use robotics as mechanism and motivation, not as core identification.
